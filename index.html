Meet Ashley Schmalz

Ashley has been professionally bettering the lives of both dogs and owners since 2013. Ashleyâ€™s love for dogs began at a young age, however, she found herself in the field after her personal dog, Twilight, began to display severe behavior issues in 2010 and was told by her veterinarian to have her put to sleep. After working Twilight through her issues, Ashley found a true passion to dog training and wanted to devote herself to helping owners who found themselves in similar circumstances.

Ashley is a LIMA (Least Invasive, Minimally Aversive) trainer whose training style is rooted in positive reinforcement and relationship building, as recommended by councils such as the CCPDT (Council of Certified Professional Dog Trainers), the APDT (Association of Pet Dog Trainers), and the IAABC (International Association of Animal Behavior Consultants); which only allows aversive as a last-option after all other methods have been exhausted. Ashley holds her CPDT-KA (Certified Professional Dog Trainer-Knowledge Assessed), CCFC (Canine Conditioning Fitness Coach) certifications and is a CGC evaluator through the AKC (American Kennel Club).

In her free time, Ashley enjoys hiking in Austin with her four dogs and spending her free time partaking in dog sports and related activities such as: Barn Hunt, Fast CAT, CABT, Disc, and more!

Private Training

In Home Lessons

Private lessons are done in the home where Ashley will work with you one-on-one to tackle even most difficult issues with you pup! For private sessions, we always start out with a single session. This gives us the opportunity, as a trainer, to properly assess your pup and let you know how many sessions it will take to reach your specific training goals! Private Lessons are 60 minutes long each and work best for long-term success as most problems we encounter with our dogs, need their owner to have a heavy involvement in during the learning process.

Day Training

Have a busy schedule but need your dog trained? Our Day Training option works best for households who already have minimal training experience with their pup, but need a jump-start in training. We take your pup and work with them in a multitude environments (in home and in public) and accomplish your training goals. During the day, we are rotating between play, rest, and training during our 3 hour session. Day Training has limited availability! Programs also come with complimentary private sessions where we have a "parent-teacher conference" to go over your pup's progress and guide you on how to maintain the behavior.
